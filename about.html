What is Yummy Tummy:
Yummy Tummy is a newly opened and established supermarket holding group. As business entity, serving customers is the heart of everything we do. We are a small organization, touching thousand of people's lives every day. This scale gives us an opportunity to make a slightly positive difference to some of the biggest challenges facing the country.


Goal:
Not long ago, shopping in Egypt has been quite a tedious, time-consuming task involving several weekly visits to a huge number of small, specialized shops. With changes in today’s lifestyle, consumers have developed a taste for quality packaged goods. To fulfill consumers’ needs, Yummy Tummy believed it was time to introduce a more convenient means of shopping in Egypt. An initial in-depth survey of the country’s shopping scene revealed that although some small supermarket's website did exist, there is plenty of room for improvement. Yummy Tummy aims to round off its distribution operation by introducing a new supermarket website.
