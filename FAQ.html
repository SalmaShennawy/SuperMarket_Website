General
After you are done scrolling and selected all your requied or wished items, select your location you like to your to be delivered to. For your first order, fill-in your address and register your mobile number After that review your order, select the preferred payment method and place your order.

With Yummy Tummy, you can order a wide range of products, from fresh fruits to party necessary. Product lines include: fruits & vegetables, dairy & eggs, bakery, soft drinks & juices, candies & snacks, meat & fish, cans & jars, pasta and rice, coffee and tea, herbs.

Make sure there are no spelling mistakes in your search words. If the product is unavailable, there is an easy way to suggest to us to add it. You can visit the side menu and tap suggest a product option. We will do our best to include it in the website as of the earliest.

Yummy Tummy does not charge its user at the time of the order being placed. The user is charged once the order is completed (out for delivery) i.e. after substitution, or taking into account any price discrepancies; hence, the amount charged is equivalent to the value of the products delivered. In any case if you are charged incorrectly, you may contact us. Yummy Tummy will initiate the refund immediately.

There are 3 methods avaible for your order payment:
  • Credit card reader on delivery
  • Cash on delivery
  • Online payment via the app.
